    Meet Jeri Rota  

 
   
         
   
     

        Founded by Jericoacoara enthusiasts, Jeri Rota was created with the purpose of offering not just transportation, but authentic and unforgettable experiences to those visiting this charming destination.      

     

        Over the years, we have established ourselves as a reference in **private transfers** and **personalized tours**, with a total focus on the safety, punctuality, and comfort of our clients. We work with modern vehicles and qualified staff, always ready to adapt itineraries to each traveler's profile.      

     

        More than transport, we deliver hospitality. Our local team knows every corner of Jeri and the region — and that’s what makes all the difference. We prioritize humanized service, respecting the environment and committed to valuing local culture.      

     

        If you are looking for a safe, organized journey full of good memories, Jeri Rota is the right choice.
